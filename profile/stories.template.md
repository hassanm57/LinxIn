> `cover-letter`, `proposal`, and `interview-prep` reuse these narratives so you
> never rewrite the same story twice.
>
> Format each story as STAR + a short Reflection. Tag it so skills can find it.

---

## [Short title] · tags: leadership, backend, incident

- **Situation:** the context and stakes.
- **Task:** what you specifically owned.
- **Action:** what YOU did (not the team) — concrete, technical, decisive.
- **Result:** the outcome, with a metric if you have one.
- **Reflection:** what you learned / would do differently. This is what makes
  the story land in interviews.
