# Local (Pakistan) CV — base

> Copy to `profile/cv-local.md` (gitignored). This is your base for DOMESTIC
> roles (Rozee, Mustakbil, local companies), which often expect a fuller format.
>
> Include here only what YOU are comfortable sharing, and only what a local
> employer legitimately needs. LinxIn will still warn you before any of this
> PII goes to an international employer or an unverified recruiter.

## Header
Name · City · Phone · Email · LinkedIn

## Objective / Summary
2–3 lines tailored to the local market.

## Skills
Grouped and keyword-rich.

## Experience
Company · Title · Dates:
- Achievement-oriented bullets with metrics where possible.

## Education
Degree · Institution · Year · (CGPA/grade if strong).

## Projects / Certifications
Relevant items with links.

## Personal details (optional — local convention)
Only if you choose to. Common locally but NOT required:
CNIC, date of birth, marital status, references. Consider leaving these off and
sharing on request. LinxIn treats all of these as sensitive PII.
