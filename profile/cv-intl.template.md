# International / ATS CV — base

> Copy to `profile/cv-intl.md` (gitignored). This is your base for GLOBAL roles.
> `tailor-cv` starts from this file and adapts it per listing.
>
> **PII rule (enforced by LinxIn):** an international CV must NEVER include a
> photo, CNIC, date of birth, marital status, religion, or gender. If any of
> those appear here, tailoring will strip them and warn you.

## Header
Name · City, Country (or "Remote — Pakistan (UTC+5)") · Email · Phone · LinkedIn · GitHub/Portfolio

## Summary
2–3 lines: who you are, your strongest specialty, and the value you bring.
Keep it results- and skills-oriented (ATS reads this).

## Skills
Grouped, keyword-rich, honest. (e.g. Languages · Frameworks · Cloud/DevOps · Data)

## Experience
For each role — Company · Title · Dates · Location/Remote:
- Bullet with an action verb, a concrete result, and a metric where possible.
- 3–6 bullets per recent role, fewer for older ones.

## Projects (optional but strong for remote/tech)
Name · one line · stack · link.

## Education
Degree · Institution · Year. (No CNIC, no DOB.)

## Certifications / Languages
Relevant certs; spoken/written languages with proficiency.
